Gift Wrapping

Fog Town Toys offers free gift wrapping year round. We can gift wrap your items for any occasion in our wide assortment of gift wrapping paper. Cards for many occasions are available for an additional cost.

Local Deliveries

Fog Town Toys offers free local delivery from Moss Beach to Half Moon Bay. A fee may apply for deliveries outside of these areas. Please inquire with any sales-associate during your transaction for current rates and schedules.

Assembly Assistance

Fog Town Toys offers free assembly on all our toys. Take the frustration out of finding the right tools and making sense of difficult directions. Let us build it for you!

UPS Shipping

Fog Town Toys offers UPS Shipping within the United States. A fee will apply based on size and/or weight. Purchase your items, have them wrapped and we can send them off to arrive in time for that special occasion.

Layaway Service

Fog Town Toys offers a six month layaway plan. A 25% deposit will hold the goods and you'll have six months to pay for your items. Our convienent plan ensures your items are in stock when you need them. Once your purchase is paid in full you can pick them up, have them shipped, delivered, or we can hold them until the day you need them.

Special Requests

From private shopping events to birthday parties, we can help with many special requests. Please contact a sales associate to discuss your special request.
